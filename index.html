The Wedding

23 - 25 May 2025

Elizabeth and Ignatius are getting married!

Our first date was on 1 October 2022, when we met in De Waal park for a walk. From there followed movie nights, hikes, Woordfees (x3), music festivals, family getaways, new friends, and a lot of laughter.

Who would have thought that, two and a half years later, an Afrikaans boy from Pretoria and a Shona girl from Mutare would be ready to spend the rest of their lives together.

We're so excited to start this new chapter, and we can't wait to share it with you.

Our wedding festivities are taking place over a weekend from Friday 23 to Sunday 25 May 2025, starting with a welcome braai on the Friday night, and ending with a farewell breakfast on the Sunday morning. We'll be in a beautiful and peaceful setting, surrounded by nature.

We invite you to join us for a weekend of connection, fun and celebration.

Venue

De Uijlenes is a working farm two hours from Cape Town, just outside Gansbaai. We've chosen this venue because it provides the space and amenities for our guests to relax and enjoy the weekend in a beautiful environment. It also features ample on-site accommodation.

Accommodation

We have secured comfortable accommodation on the venue for the weekend, at R400 per person per night. Each room has its own bathroom, and full self-catering facilities are available. You can also order breakfast from the chef on Saturday morning.

Please confirm on the RSVP form you received via email if you would like to book.

Little ones

A number of our guests have young children, and we would love for them to join us for the weekend!

During the ceremony and reception we will have a children's area set up with games and activities, and a trusted babysitter to look after them.
